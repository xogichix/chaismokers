The CHAI profession

The Indian tea industry is worth more than 18000 Cr., sealing the tea segment the highest share in non-alcoholic drinks. Indians consume about 837,000 tonnes of CHAI every year. Tea is cheaper than coffee in terms of production, price and selling. India has a younger populataion as an urbanization effect, millennials are the largest cadre of the consumer. The current global tea market size is around $56 billion and is estimated to be at $87 billion in 2026. Supermarkets/malls/hypermarkets have the highest share in tea distribution. Digital India push by the government forces users to move towards online purchase as and when convenient, and the tea segment has registered the fastest growth in online selling (VAGR ~ 8%). Most tea brands are now creating their speciality stores e.g., CCD,Chai Point, Wagh Bakri Tea, etc.
